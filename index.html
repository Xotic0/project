About EPSF ANU

EPSF ANU (Egyptian Pharmaceutical Students' Federation — Alexandria National University) is a student-led community founded in 2021 dedicated to public health awareness, professional development, and community service. We host workshops, campaigns, and events that connect students with healthcare professionals and the public.

  • Founded: 2021
  • Focus: Events, Public Health, Student Development
  • Committees: PH, PE, Marketing, HR, SEC GEN, Treasure, LOC
