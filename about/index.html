About EPSF-ANU

EPSF-ANU was founded in 2021 with the mission to empower pharmacy students through advocacy, education, and public health campaigns. We organize events, workshops, and community outreach to develop professional and leadership skills.

Our Vision

To be a leading student organization that shapes future pharmacists with strong public health commitment.

What we do

  • Organize awareness campaigns (public health & disease prevention)
  • Provide educational workshops & professional development
  • Support student projects and community service
  • Host the Annual Open General Assembly and events
